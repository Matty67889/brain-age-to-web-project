Low to High

Instructions

Once the game begins, numbers will appear on the screen for a couple seconds, and then turn into boxes. The goal is to click on the boxes in order of least to greatest number. If a box is chosen in the incorrect order, the round ends.

About

This game is a recreation of the game "Low to High" from the game Brain Age for the Nintendo DS. I do not claim any ownership of the original game. This version will allow users to customize the rules of the game, deviating from the original in this way. I was inspired to create this because I didn't see any online versions of Brain Age games on the Internet.
